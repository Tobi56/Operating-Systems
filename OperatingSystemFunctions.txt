One aspect of an OS is device management. The device management displays all devices connected to the computer. It also controls programs that connect to ceratin devices.
Another function is security. The OS keeps the user's data safe by using password protection. It keeps out people that shouldn't be on your computer.
A third function is process management. The OS will look over the status of processes. It will look over every program that is currently running and give certain ones top priority over another program.
